Most people are waiting. For the kids to grow up. For work to calm down. For the right moment.

The Live It List is what you do instead of waiting.

What it is

Simple. Honest.
Entirely yours.

A Live It List is the things you want to do, feel, and live - written down, with one honest question to come back to:

"Am I living it?"

Just your list, your life, and one question that changes how you look at both.

Things that might be on a Live It List...

  • ✦ Stop saying yes when I mean no
  • ✦Get outside more
  • ✦ Write the thing I keep not writing
  • ✦ More mornings with nowhere to be
  • ✦ Read the book that's been on my shelf for months
  • ✦ Go for that coffee with a friend

From Diane

"I created the Live It List because I was tired of bucket lists. Skydiving, climbing mountains, quitting everything and moving abroad."

I just wanted to go for coffee with a friend. Walk somewhere. Spend a Sunday the way I actually wanted to spend it. Make memories with the people I love without waiting for the perfect occasion.

So I wrote it down. The small things. The simple things. The things that make an ordinary day feel like mine.

Diane Corriette
