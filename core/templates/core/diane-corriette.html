About Diane Corriette

I live what I teach. I have self-authored my life - which means I stopped letting other people's expectations write my story. Instead I started making intentional choices aligned with what actually matters to me. It was a deliberate process of noticing where I was living in default mode, and choosing differently.

I am not here as someone with all the answers. I'm here as someone who has lived through enough to speak from experience.

Now, I help women stop waiting and start making intentional choices about how they live, feel, and earn - so they can stop living in default mode and finally choose their own direction.
