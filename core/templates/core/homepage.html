Most people are waiting. For the kids to grow up. For work to calm down. For the right moment.

And somewhere along the way, your days start to blur into one another.

A Live It List is what you do instead of waiting.

What it is

Simple. Honest.
Entirely yours.

A Live It List is a list of small things that make you feel more alive, with one honest question to come back to:

"Am I living it?"

Not because any single moment changes your life, but because repeated acts of aliveness change how life feels.

Things that might be on a Live It List...

  • Visit a bookshop
  • Take a different route home
  • Watch the sun rise
  • More mornings with nowhere to be
  • Eat lunch on a park bench
  • Go for that coffee with a friend

From Diane

"I created A Live It List because I was tired of bucket lists. Skydiving, climbing mountains, quitting everything and moving abroad."

I spent years managing a busy freelance business, family, and my own daily loops. I just wanted a simple way to feel alive inside my actual, everyday life. So I wrote it down. The small things. The simple things. The things that make an ordinary day feel like mine.

I taught myself what was needed to disrupt the monotony and pull me out of my rut. I built this space for you to do the same.

Diane Corriette
