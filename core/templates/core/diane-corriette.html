About Diane Corriette

For most of my younger life I kept quiet, went along with things, and called it keeping the peace. Now, I live what I teach.

I have self-authored my life - which means I stopped letting other people's expectations write my story. Instead I started making intentional choices aligned with what actually matters to me. It was a deliberate process of noticing where I was living in default mode, and choosing differently.

I help quiet women do the same. Stop waiting and start making intentional choices about your life, so you can finally choose your own direction.

Using AI

I enjoy using AI as part of the way I work and create. It helps me save time, develop ideas more quickly, and spend more of my energy on the parts of the work I care about most.

I use AI to support content writing, image creation, and video development. It allows me to bring ideas to life more easily while still keeping the message, direction, and final decisions in my hands.

Personal Growth Coach

I am a Personal Growth Coach and the creator behind Inspirational Guidance. I bring decades of experience in coaching and mentoring, NLP, and personal development, backed by professional certifications and practical tools.

My work today brings these skills together in a more practical, grounded way — focused on helping women move forward with clarity and intention.

I am the creator of the PAUSE Framework — a five-step emotional resilience method built from my coaching work and personal practice. PAUSE stands for Pause, Accept, Understand, Strengthen, and Evolve. It is used by women navigating stress, setbacks, and difficult transitions who want to respond rather than react.

I am an INFJ introvert. A 29/11 path walker and a 6/2 Role Model/Hermit — Pure Generator — Human Design. What does all that mean? I was born to guide people towards transformation.

What I Write About

Every guide, article, and tool on Inspirational Guidance connects back to one core idea: that women deserve a life they have deliberately chosen. These are the six topic areas I write about — each grounded in my coaching training, NLP practice, and lived experience.

Self-Authorship

How to define your own beliefs, identity, and direction — rather than living by someone else's script. The central thread of my work.

Emotional Resilience — the PAUSE Framework

My own five-step framework for responding to stress and setbacks with calm and clarity. Created from over two decades of coaching experience.

Build Self-Confidence

Confidence as a practised skill — built through clarity, language, small actions, and evidence. Not motivation. Not affirmations. Real, repeatable steps.

Live With Purpose

Purpose as a daily practice of values-led choices — not a grand calling to locate once. How to align your everyday decisions with what genuinely matters.

Empowered Living

Boundaries, self-reliance, confident decisions, and turning awareness into action. Practical tools for women ready to take ownership of their lives.

Journaling for Personal Growth

A 10-minute daily practice for clarity, resilience, confidence, and self-knowledge. I have used journaling as a personal tool for over two decades.

Living Out Loud Definition

Living out loud means living your life authentically, unapologetically, and openly according to your own values and passions rather than conforming to the expectations of others. It is the practice of being yourself fully and sharing that identity fearlessly with the world.
