Upload a CSV file to bulk-create draft keyword pages. After import, select the pages you want to process, choose "Set template and generate content" from the Actions menu, and paste your template.

CSV columns

keyword *The topic word or phrase. Slug is auto-generated.
meta_titleOptional. Max 60 chars.
meta_descriptionOptional. Max 160 chars.
cta_textOptional. Max 200 chars.
cta_urlOptional. Full URL for the CTA button.

Rows where the slug already exists will be skipped and reported as duplicates. All imported pages are created as Draft.
